Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a car?

Booking a car is easy! Simply browse our available cars, select your preferred dates and times, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card for payment, and proof of insurance. International renters may need additional documentation.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before your pickup time. Cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing and terms of your booking.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns are subject to additional charges. We recommend contacting us as soon as possible if you anticipate being late to discuss your options.

Is insurance included?

Basic insurance is included with all rentals. Additional coverage options are available for purchase to provide extra protection during your rental period.

How do I become a car owner on Rentify?

To become a car owner, simply register with the "owner" role, complete your profile, and start listing your cars. Our team will review your listing before it goes live.
